About Elon Musk

Elon Musk is a South African-born American entrepreneur and business magnate who co-founded PayPal and Tesla Motors. He is also the CEO and lead designer at SpaceX, and the founder of The Boring Company and Neuralink. Musk is known for his ambitious goals and projects, such as colonizing Mars and revolutionizing the transportation industry with electric cars and high-speed underground tunnels.
